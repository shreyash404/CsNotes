Why computer organization and design is important in computer science include:

It helps to understand how computers work: By studying computer organization and design, you can learn about the various hardware and software components that make up a computer system, as well as how they work together to execute programs and perform various tasks.


Overall, understanding computer organization and design is crucial for designing and building effective computer systems, as well as for developing new technologies that push the boundaries of what is possible with computers.
